===IMPORTANT===
Kson&Friends is a content mod that adds Kson the Delinquent Vtuber and her friends to your world! 
###THIS MOD IS STILL IN DEVELOPMENT. NEW CONTENT WILL BE ADDED IN THE FORM OF UPDATE PATCHES.###
Please report bugs/issues/improvements to @v_elisaya on Twitter.


Kson Friends (un)officially joining Terraria! Bringing alone they own character exclusive weapons and tools that will make your journey easier.


This mod -
- Adds 17 new friendly NPCs.
- Adds 19 new weapon/tool/mount.
- Adds 2 new mounts.
- Adds 1 wings.
